Welcome to Qubic!

To begin, hit play. You will enter the Qubic Zone (QZ). In the QZ, there are three dimensions. Qubic is Tic-Tac-Toe meets Connect Four meets THE THIRD DIMENSION.

RULES:

1. You and another player will alternate turns placing pieces on any un-claimed spots on the board.

2. If a player achieves four contiguous pieces placed, they win!

3. Remember to watch the diagonals- you can win across all dimensions.

Good luck!
